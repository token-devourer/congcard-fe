# Beginner Guide

## Goal

Be the first player to empty your hand. Each round awards points based on the cards left in other players' hands.

## How a Turn Works

On your turn, play one card that matches the active color, the number, or the symbol on the discard pile. You can also play a Wild card.

If you cannot play, draw one card. If the drawn card can be played, you may play it right away or pass.

## Action Cards

- Skip: the next player loses a turn.
- Reverse: the turn order changes direction. With two players, it acts like Skip.
- Draw Two: the next player draws two cards and loses a turn.
- Wild: choose the next active color.
- Wild Draw Four: choose a color and make the next player draw four cards unless they challenge successfully.

## Calling One

When you have one card left, press **One!** quickly. If you forget, another player can catch you and you draw two cards.

## Quick Tips

- Save Wild cards for moments when you need control.
- Watch opponent card counts.
- Call **One!** as soon as you drop to one card.
- With many players, pay attention to the turn timer.
